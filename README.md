# tophat
A sophisticated take on quantum operators.

This is very much a work in progress, and not suitable for human or animal consumption.


## Purpose

The goal of Top Hat is to explore more efficient methods of operator construction and manipulation than currently exist in Qiskit.  In addition, fast matrix-vector product operations, either in terms of sparse matrix or matrix-free methods, is in scope.  This is aided by transformations using extended alphabets.

## Goals

- Have some fun
- Explore numerical methods on quantum operators
- Publish a paper perhaps

## What in the world is Fulqrum?

The word Fulqrum is a take on fulcrum, and is meant to decribe "software for *leveraging* quantum computers".  It is used here for a grouping of projects that hopefully will see the light of day.
